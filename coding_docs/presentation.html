AI Product Buyer

End-to-end agentic commerce: describe what you need, and an AI agent handles discovery, ranking, cart, and checkout across multiple retailers.

HackNation 2025 · VC Track · 24-Hour Build

The Problem

Shopping is Broken

🔍

Fragmented Search

Users bounce between tabs and retailers, manually comparing prices, delivery times, and product options across dozens of sites.

⏱

Time-Consuming

A multi-item purchase (outfit, party supplies, gear) takes hours of repetitive searching, filtering, and comparing.

🛒

Separate Checkouts

Each retailer requires its own cart, payment entry, and shipping form. There's no unified buying experience.

❓

No Transparency

Existing tools rank by ad spend, not value. Users can't see why a product is recommended over another.

The Solution

Conversational Agentic Commerce

  • Describe your shopping needs in plain language — budget, deadline, preferences, must-haves
  • AI agent searches 3+ retailers in real time, extracting structured product data from live pages
  • Products ranked with a transparent, explainable weighted scoring system — not a black box
  • Unified multi-store cart with budget tracking, item swapping, and automatic re-optimization
  • Single checkout flow: enter payment and address once, agent orchestrates per-retailer orders

"I need a full Super Bowl outfit in team style, budget $150, delivered by Friday." → Agent handles the rest.

2

Tavily adapter with retailer diversity enforcement — guarantees 3+ unique retailers

3

ScrapeService

Fetches product pages, extracts structured ProductCandidate data via hybrid pipeline

4

RankingService

Transparent weighted scoring: deterministic cost/delivery + LLM preference/coherence

5

CartService

Multi-retailer cart CRUD — add, remove, swap, lock, and re-optimize items

6

CheckoutService

Generates per-retailer simulated checkout plans with autofill previews and order confirmations

7

RunManager

Orchestrates concurrency control, rate-limit handling, retry logic, and SSE progress streaming across all item pipelines

Core Innovation

Transparent Ranking Engine

Dimension Weight Computation Type
Cost 0.30 1 - (price / itemBudget), capped at 0 Deterministic
Delivery 0.25 1.0 if on-time, penalized proportionally if late Deterministic
Preference 0.30 LLM-scored match to user's stated preferences LLM-Scored
Coherence 0.15 LLM-scored cross-item compatibility LLM-Scored
totalScore = Σ(weighti × scorei)

"Ranked #1 because: lowest price in budget (cost: 0.9), arrives 2 days early (delivery: 1.0), exact color match (preference: 0.85), pairs well with the selected jacket (coherence: 0.8)."
